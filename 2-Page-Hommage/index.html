Who is Ronaldo

Ronaldo Nazário, better known as Ronaldo, is a retired Brazilian footballer who is considered one of the greatest strikers of all time. Born on 18 September 1976 in Rio de Janeiro, he had a prolific career, playing for clubs such as Barcelona, ​​Inter Milan, Real Madrid and AC Milan. He was known for his incredible dribbling ability, speed and clinical finishing. Ronaldo won two FIFA World Cups with Brazil in 1994 and 2002 and was awarded the FIFA World Player of the Year award three times (1996, 1997 and 2002). His career has been marred by injuries, but he remains a beloved figure in the football world.
